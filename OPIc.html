Transitional Words

1. Addition (추가)

물론입니다!


- **Meaning:** In addition; as an extra or further consideration. (추가적으로; 또 다른 고려 사항으로)

- **For example:** The new model is faster. Additionally, it consumes less power.



- **Meaning:** In addition to what has been said; furthermore. (앞서 말한 것에 더하여; 게다가)

- **For example:** The plan is expensive. Moreover, it lacks public support.



- **Meaning:** In addition; continuing from what has been said. (추가적으로; 앞서 언급된 것에 이어서)

- **For example:** The project will benefit the community. Furthermore, it will create jobs.



- **Meaning:** Used to introduce an additional point that reinforces the previous statement. (이전 진술을 강화하는 추가 사항을 도입할 때 사용)

- **For example:** The trip was expensive, not to mention the time it took to get there.



- **Meaning:** In addition; too. (또한; 더불어)

- **For example:** She is a talented musician. She also paints beautifully.



- **Meaning:** In addition to something; as well as that. (어떤 것에 더하여; 또한)

- **For example:** He was late for the meeting. On top of that, he forgot his notes.



- **Meaning:** Used to introduce an additional argument or point. (추가적인 논점이나 사항을 소개할 때 사용)

- **For example:** We need to cut costs. Another point is that we should improve efficiency.



- **Meaning:** Used to add further information that emphasizes the previous statement. (이전 진술을 강조하는 추가 정보를 덧붙일 때 사용)

- **For example:** The service was slow. What’s more, the food was cold.



- **Meaning:** Used to introduce an additional fact or point. (추가적인 사실이나 사항을 도입할 때 사용)

- **For example:** The weather was terrible, as well as that, the roads were blocked.



- **Meaning:** Used to introduce something extra or further. (추가적인 내용을 도입할 때 사용)

- **For example:** The book is informative. In addition, it's well-written.



- **Meaning:** Apart from; in addition to. (~ 외에; 추가적으로)

- **For example:** I don’t feel like going out. Besides, I have a lot of work to do.



- **Meaning:** As well as; in addition. (또한; 추가적으로)

- **For example:** The new phone is cheaper. Plus, it has a better camera.



- **Meaning:** Used to add another point or argument. (또 다른 점이나 주장을 덧붙일 때 사용)

- **For example:** The room is too small. And another thing, it's too noisy.



- **Meaning:** Used to introduce additional information. (추가적인 정보를 도입할 때 사용)

- **For example:** The software is user-friendly. To add to this, it’s also very fast.



- **Meaning:** In addition to something; together with. (~와 함께; ~에 더하여)

- **For example:** She brought her laptop along with a notebook.



- **Meaning:** In a similar manner; in the same way. (비슷한 방식으로; 같은 맥락에서)

- **For example:** The first movie was a hit. In the same vein, the sequel was also successful.



- **Meaning:** In the same way; similarly. (같은 방식으로; 비슷하게)

- **For example:** She offered her help. Likewise, her colleagues were also supportive.


2. Contrast (대조)


- **Meaning:** Used to introduce a statement that contrasts with or seems to contradict something that has been said previously. (그러나; 하지만)

- **For example:** The weather was cold; however, we still went hiking.



- **Meaning:** Used to introduce a contrasting point of view, fact, or situation. (다른 한편으로는; 반면에)

- **For example:** He loves chocolate. On the other hand, his brother dislikes it.



- **Meaning:** Used to show a difference between two things. (대조적으로)

- **For example:** The south of the country is hot. In contrast, the north is very cold.



- **Meaning:** Introducing a statement or idea which reverses one that has just been made or referred to. (반대로; 역으로)

- **For example:** He prefers a quiet evening at home. Conversely, she enjoys going out.



- **Meaning:** As an alternative or substitute. (대신에)

- **For example:** We didn’t go to the park; instead, we stayed home and watched a movie.



- **Meaning:** In spite of that; despite what has just been said. (그럼에도 불구하고)

- **For example:** It was raining, but they went hiking nevertheless.



- **Meaning:** In spite of that; nevertheless. (그럼에도 불구하고)

- **For example:** The journey was difficult, but it was worth it nonetheless.



- **Meaning:** In contrast or comparison with the fact that. (~에 반하여; ~인데 반해)

- **For example:** He likes action movies, whereas his wife prefers comedies.



- **Meaning:** Used to contrast two different things or situations. (~하는 반면에)

- **For example:** She was studying while her friends were out partying.



- **Meaning:** Despite the fact that. (비록 ~일지라도)

- **For example:** Even though it was raining, they decided to go for a walk.



- **Meaning:** In spite of the fact that; even though. (비록 ~이지만)

- **For example:** Although he was tired, he continued working late into the night.



- **Meaning:** Without being affected by; in spite of. (~에도 불구하고)

- **For example:** Despite the heavy traffic, we arrived on time.



- **Meaning:** Despite; without being affected by. (~에도 불구하고)

- **For example:** She succeeded in spite of many obstacles.



- **Meaning:** Used to intensify a denial of what has just been implied or stated. (그와는 반대로)

- **For example:** He didn’t fail the test; on the contrary, he passed with flying colors.



- **Meaning:** Being different or distinct from. (~와는 다르게)

- **For example:** Her opinion is differing from the majority of the group.


3. Comparison (비교)


- **Meaning:** In a similar way; likewise. (유사하게; 마찬가지로)

- **For example:** She loves reading; similarly, her sister is always with a book.



- **Meaning:** In a manner similar to something else. (같은 방식으로)

- **For example:** He completed the project in the same way as he did the last one.



- **Meaning:** In exactly the same way as. (마치 ~처럼)

- **For example:** Just as the sun rises in the east, so too does it set in the west.



- **Meaning:** In the same way as; similar to. (마치 ~처럼)

- **For example:** He dances just like a professional.



- **Meaning:** In relation to something else; in contrast with. (~에 비해)

- **For example:** The new model is more efficient compared to the older version.



- **Meaning:** When compared to something else. (비교해보면)

- **For example:** In comparison to last year, this year’s sales have increased.



- **Meaning:** To the same degree or level; similarly. (동등하게; 똑같이)

- **For example:** She is equally talented in both singing and dancing.



- **Meaning:** For the same reason; in a similar way. (같은 이유로; 마찬가지로)

- **For example:** You shouldn’t trust him; by the same token, you shouldn’t trust his friends either.



- **Meaning:** Very similar to; in the same way as. (매우 ~와 같은)

- **For example:** The situation is much like what happened last year.



- **Meaning:** In a way that is alike or comparable. (비슷한 방식으로)

- **For example:** The two artists work in a similar manner, focusing on realism.



- **Meaning:** Looking or being similar to something else. (~을 닮은)

- **For example:** The sculpture is resembling a famous historical figure.



- **Meaning:** In a way that is similar or analogous. (상응하여; 유사하게)

- **For example:** As their responsibilities increased, their salaries were raised correspondingly.



- **Meaning:** At the same time and in a similar way. (동시에; 유사하게)

- **For example:** The two projects are progressing in parallel.



- **Meaning:** In the same way; similarly. (같은 방식으로; 비슷하게)

- **For example:** She was very happy with the results; likewise, her team was thrilled.



- **Meaning:** Comparable in certain respects, typically in a way that makes clearer the nature of the things compared. (~에 유사한)

- **For example:** The structure of the human brain is analogous to a complex computer.


4. Cause and Effect (원인과 결과)


- **Meaning:** For the reason that; since. (~때문에)

- **For example:** She stayed home because she was feeling ill.



- **Meaning:** Due to; on account of. (~때문에)

- **For example:** The event was canceled because of the bad weather.



- **Meaning:** Caused by; as a result of. (~로 인해)

- **For example:** The flight was delayed due to technical problems.



- **Meaning:** Because of; on account of. (~때문에)

- **For example:** Owing to the heavy traffic, we were late.



- **Meaning:** For the reason that; because. (~때문에)

- **For example:** Since it’s raining, the match will be postponed.



- **Meaning:** Because; for the reason that. (~때문에)

- **For example:** As he was late, the meeting started without him.



- **Meaning:** Because of this; for this cause. (이러한 이유로)

- **For example:** She didn’t study hard. For this reason, she failed the exam.



- **Meaning:** Because of; due to. (~때문에)

- **For example:** The match was postponed on account of the rain.



- **Meaning:** Considering the fact that; because. (~을 고려하여)

- **For example:** Seeing that it’s already late, we should leave now.



- **Meaning:** Taking into account; in view of. (~을 고려할 때)

- **For example:** Considering the circumstances, he handled the situation well.



- **Meaning:** For that reason; as a result. (따라서)

- **For example:** The team worked hard; therefore, they won the game.



- **Meaning:** Consequently; because of this. (그 결과)

- **For example:** The company cut costs; as a result, their profits increased.



- **Meaning:** As a result; therefore. (그 결과)

- **For example:** She missed the deadline; consequently, her project was delayed.



- **Meaning:** As a result or consequence of this; therefore. (따라서)

- **For example:** The experiment failed; thus, the hypothesis was incorrect.



- **Meaning:** For this reason; consequently. (이 때문에)

- **For example:** He was the eldest son; hence, he inherited the title.



- **Meaning:** For that reason; therefore. (그래서)

- **For example:** It was late, so they decided to go home.



- **Meaning:** Because of this; therefore. (이러한 이유로)

- **For example:** He was unwell. For this reason, he didn't attend the meeting.



- **Meaning:** As a result; in a way that is appropriate to the particular circumstances. (따라서)

- **For example:** She was promoted and was accordingly given more responsibilities.



- **Meaning:** Causes or results in something. (이것이 ~로 이어지다)

- **For example:** Lack of sleep leads to decreased productivity.



- **Meaning:** As a result; therefore. (그 결과로)

- **For example:** The weather was terrible; as a consequence, the event was canceled.


5. Conclusion (결론)


- **Meaning:** Used to introduce a final statement or summary. (결론적으로)

- **For example:** In conclusion, the project was a great success.



- **Meaning:** To summarize; to present a brief summary. (요약하자면)

- **For example:** To sum up, we achieved all our goals for the year.



- **Meaning:** Briefly stating the main points. (요약하면)

- **For example:** In summary, the study showed significant improvements.



- **Meaning:** To bring something to an end; to summarize. (결론적으로)

- **For example:** To conclude, the evidence supports our hypothesis.



- **Meaning:** To give a brief statement of the main points. (요약하자면)

- **For example:** To summarize, the results were better than expected.



- **Meaning:** Considering everything; on the whole. (전반적으로)

- **For example:** All in all, it was a successful event.



- **Meaning:** In the end; finally. (궁극적으로)

- **For example:** Ultimately, the decision rests with the board of directors.



- **Meaning:** At the end; after a long time. (마지막으로)

- **For example:** Finally, I would like to thank everyone for their hard work.



- **Meaning:** Ultimately; after everything has been considered. (결국)

- **For example:** In the end, the team managed to meet the deadline.



- **Meaning:** Briefly; in a few words. (간단히 말해서)

- **For example:** In short, the plan needs more work.



- **Meaning:** Considering everything; generally. (전체적으로)

- **For example:** On the whole, the project was a success.



- **Meaning:** To introduce the last and usually the most important point. (마지막으로)

- **For example:** As a final point, we need to focus on customer satisfaction.



- **Meaning:** In short; briefly. (간단히 말해)

- **For example:** In brief, the findings confirm our initial hypothesis.



- **Meaning:** Essentially; in a basic or fundamental way. (본질적으로)

- **For example:** In essence, the problem lies in communication.



- **Meaning:** Taking everything into account; generally. (전반적으로)

- **For example:** Overall, the results were positive.
